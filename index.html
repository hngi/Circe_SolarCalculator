About The App

The Solar App calculator is an app which will help you to calculate the total solar power needed to power your household appliances

How it works

  • Electronic type: This refers to the electronic device you want to know the total power needed. It could be your refrigerator, iron, cooker, bulb
  • Quantity here refers to the quantity of electricity needed which is the quantity of charge. This can be calculated by multiplying the current passing through the time. And sometimes it could be written on the device the quanity of electricity it could consume
  • Hour of use per day is the number of hours you use this particular device in a day
  • Power consumption is the total power the device could consume. It is usually written on the device. It is measured in watts(W)
  • Battery Voltage is measured in volts(V)
  • Solar panel wattage is the total power needed to power the solar panel which is measured in watts(W)
  • Battery capacity is measured in Milliamperes per hour
  • Hours of sunlight used per day is the total amount of sunlight used to charge the device

Now that each term has been defined, all you have to do now is to input the necessary values, click on calculate and the total solar power needed will be given.
